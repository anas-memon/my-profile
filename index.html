About

Anas is a first-year student at ESSEC Business School, France. Born in India and raised in and around the Middle East, Anas strives to embody the principles of a global citizen. Now 20, he spends his time in Dubai split between coursework, daytrading, reading, and spending time with friends and family.

Interests

Anas is passionate about, and engaged in social entrepreneurship, trading in financial markets, continental philosophy, and the pinnacle of sports: football. Every year until Year 0 of the pandemic, Anas made sure to travel to a new country, even if for a little bit - to experience and learn from diverse cultural phenomenon. He enjoys the occasional gaming session, and cherishes long drives and trips to the beach.

Professional Experiences

Anas delved into his professional career at 16, with an internship in Re-insurance. Since then, he has interned in Data Analysis and Investment Banking, whilst winning nation-wide awards from Liv Bank. Anas held a year-long post as the writer for an esteemed admissions group, drafting admissions essays for undergraduate and master's students aspiring to travel worldwide. Additionally, and most importantly, his most rewarding experience was the conception of an NPA with his friends at the age of 15. This organization ran smoothly for 4 years, and financed the education of underprivileged children and maintanence of labor camps in 'third-world' countries.
